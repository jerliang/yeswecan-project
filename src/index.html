Our Mission
We believe that it's never to early to teach kids about safety. As the saying goes, it's better to be safe than sorry.

According to the California Department of Conservation, our state has between two to three major earthquakes each year that destroys buildings.

California is also known for its devastating wildfires and we don't want kids to get caught in the crossfire.

We provide a resource hub of interactive games that will teach your children what to do in case of a disaster. They're educational and fun :)
